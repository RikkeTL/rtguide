The Country

Denmark is a country with a rich history of design, collaboration, and work-life balance. Values that resonate deeply with how we work at Doist. As a remote-first company, we believe in the Danish concept of hygge: creating warmth, togetherness, and a sense of belonging wherever we are.

With its stunning coastlines, world-class cities, and a culture that prioritises quality of life, Denmark offers the perfect backdrop for a week of focused work, meaningful connections, and shared experiences.

10 Fun Facts about Denmark

  • 🇩🇰 The Danish flag (Dannebrog) is the world's oldest national flag still in use, dating back to 1219 (according to legend).
  • 🏝️ Denmark has 406 islands, of which 74 are inhabited.
  • 🚲 There are around 5.8 million bicycles in Denmark — almost one for every resident.
  • 🌊 No point in Denmark is more than 52 km (32 miles) from the sea.
  • 👑 The Danish monarchy dates back more than 1,000 years, making it one of the oldest in Europe.
  • 💨 Around 60% of Denmark's electricity comes from wind power, one of the highest shares in the world.
  • 😊 Denmark has ranked #1 in the World Happiness Report three times (2013, 2016, and 2024) and is almost always in the top five.
  • 🎂 The oldest amusement park still operating in the world, Bakken, opened in 1583, just north of Copenhagen.
  • 🌳 More than 15% of Denmark is covered by forest, compared with around 2–3% 200 years ago due to large-scale reforestation.
  • ☕ The average Dane drinks around 4 cups of coffee per day, placing Denmark among the world's biggest coffee-consuming nations.

The Venue

We chose a venue that inhabits the best parts of venues where we've had successful retreats in the past: beautiful, with a rich history, surrounded by nature, and of course, has a great spa! Set against the Danish landscape, Hotel Vejlefjord gives every team the room they need to do their best work together, while shared spaces make it easy to connect with colleagues across the company.
