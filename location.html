Why this location?

Denmark 2026 - why we chose this country and venue for our Doist Connect.

The Country

Denmark is a country with a rich history of design, collaboration, and work-life balance - values that resonate deeply with how we work at Doist. As a remote-first company, we believe in the Danish concept of hygge: creating warmth, togetherness, and a sense of belonging wherever we are. Denmark felt like the natural home for our first Doist Connect.

With its stunning coastlines, world-class cities, and a culture that prioritises quality of life, Denmark offers the perfect backdrop for a week of focused work, meaningful connections, and shared experiences.

The Venue

We chose a venue that reflects the spirit of Doist - beautiful, thoughtfully designed, and built for both deep work and genuine connection. Set against the Danish landscape, our retreat space gives every team the room they need to do their best work together, while shared spaces make it easy to connect with colleagues across the company.

A Personal Connection

Denmark also holds a special place in our story. Our CEO Amir grew up here, and this Doist Connect is a chance to experience a place that shaped him - and in many ways, shaped Doist too.
